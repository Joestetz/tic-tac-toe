An AngularJS tic-tac-toe game

About:

A simple game of tic-tac-toe built with AngularJS. Fill out the player names and take turns placing your game piece on the board. The first player to get all three of their game pieces in a row (vertical, horizontal, or diagonal) wins!

To reset the game, click the 'New Game' button at the top of the screen.

Ready? Click Play in the top navigation to start your game
